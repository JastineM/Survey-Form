Her

Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson was born on 15 April 1990 in Paris, to English lawyers Chris Watson and Jacqueline Luesby. Watson lived in Maisons-Laffitte near Paris until age five. Her parents divorced when she was young, and Watson moved to England to live with her mother in Oxfordshire while spending weekends at her father's house in London.Watson has said she speaks some French, though "not as well" as she used to.After moving to Oxford with her mother and brother, she attended the Dragon School, remaining there until 2003. From age six, she wanted to become an actress, and trained at the Oxford branch of Stagecoach Theatre Arts, a part-time theatre school where she studied singing, dancing, and acting.


Emma Watson is best known for her portrayal of Hermione in the Harry Potter series film. But over the years, people have witnessed as she grows as a versatile actress and assumes role beyond the film industry. In her most recent engagements, media continues to follow her as she becomes more vocal about issues concerning gender equality. For more information, you may visit this link.

Actress

She has won eight Teen Choice Awards, three MTVS Movie Awards and had been nominated for various other awards throughout her career.

Activist

She was appointed UN Women Goodwill Ambassador. She also started the campaign HeforShe which encourages men to support the feminist movement.

Writer

She graduated from the Brown University and authored articles that had circulated in international news outlet.
